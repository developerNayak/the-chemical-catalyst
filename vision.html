Vision and Strategies

Vision

Making chemistry accessible for all

Strategies

  • Conducting research to find out what is happening in the field and present it in an easy-to-understand format. This will ensure global engagement by encouraging to look at the work being done in the subject to even try to do something similar.

  • Including fun activities and visual elements would better explain the concepts along with study tips and experiments to show the concepts work in a laboratory and real-life along with their impacts. This would ensure the ethics of choices and actions by using a sustainable and socially responsible approach towards the subject.

  • Using the right language based of the purpose of the audience to study the subject and their level of understanding would allow a further work on their strengths and growth by using existing skills to take up new challenges.

  • Giving the right knowledge would develop confidence to be able to speak about it openly in a way that everybody understands.

  • We will ask for feedback and take suggestions on a timely basis so that we can adjust our material according to changing preferences and update it regularly. We would even classify information so that everyone can find and see what is suitable for them.

All this would require self-initiative and planning by using our resources to enhance the understanding of the subject.

Our main goal is to try and modify our resources and content based on needs and requirements of viewers.

The Chemical Catalyst

A Platform where chemistry lovers and others who are interested can find a wide range of subject-related resources according to their needs and requirements

information

The Chemical Catalyst is a platform that provides an opportunity to build essential skills in a fun way along with becoming aware of the global issues and what is happening around us to be able to solve them using our knowledge of Chemistry. This enables us to change the world and helps in pursuing a career in the subject. During this process a life-long passion for learning would be developed in us and enabling us to become dynamic individuals.
